Solarly.ai — Disclosures & Project Status

Solarly.ai is currently a private hobby / research prototype focused on risk controls, auditability, and reporting for automated portfolio actions. It is not a regulated financial service and is not offered for public client use.

Nothing on this website is investment advice, a recommendation, or a solicitation to buy or sell securities or digital assets.

Current status (R&D / hobby)

Solarly.ai is a hobby / R&D project until it is legally structured, licensed/registered as required, and independently reviewed for security and compliance.

What Solarly does (today)

  • Risk policies (caps, allowed universe, kill switch)
  • Audit logs (who/what/why)
  • Reporting (daily summaries, exceptions)

What Solarly does not do (today)

  • Provide investment advice
  • Accept client funds or custody assets
  • Trade on behalf of the public
  • Promise performance or outcomes

Path to launch (intent)

  1. R&D / simulation-only
  2. Compliance-forward build (controls, security, reviews)
  3. Legally structured go-to-market (as required, before broad use)
